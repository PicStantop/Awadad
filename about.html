About Awadad Medical Laboratory and Diagnostics Center

At Awadad Medical Laboratory and Diagnostics Center, we are committed to delivering accurate, timely, and affordable diagnostic testing services for individuals, families, and healthcare providers. With a foundation built on precision, professionalism, and care, our goal is to support your health decisions with dependable laboratory results you can trust. Located in a modern, clean, and easily accessible facility, Awadad Laboratory serves both walk-in patients and partner clinics, offering a broad range of diagnostic services. From routine blood work to advanced microbiology, our lab is fully equipped with state-of-the-art technology and operated by licensed professionals trained to uphold the highest standards of medical testing.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, accurate, and timely diagnostic services that empower individuals and support clinical decision-making for healthier communities.

Our Vision

To become the leading diagnostic laboratory in the region, recognized for our quality, integrity, and innovation in medical testing.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Accuracy You Can Rely On: Every result is double-checked, validated, and produced using certified equipment.
  • Fast Turnaround: We know time is critical. Our streamlined systems ensure you get results as quickly as possible.
  • Affordable Pricing: We believe everyone deserves access to medical testing without financial strain.
  • Friendly Service: Our team of medical scientists, lab technicians, and customer care agents work with empathy and professionalism.

Who We Serve

Individual walk-in patients | Families and caregivers | Clinics, private hospitals, and corporate organizations | Pre-employment & wellness test clients | Schools and community health initiatives

Our Facility

Our lab is designed with patient comfort and operational efficiency in mind. From specimen collection to result dispatch, our process is clean, confidential, and patient-focused.
